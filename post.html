Tea Mitten

Tuesday March 9, 2021

The Tea Mitten was a pattern and yarn my aunt had bought for me many years ago. I decided to finally knit this project during the Covid-19 pandemic. It turned out great! However, I did this backwards. I knit the tea mitten before I had a teapot! It's also very difficult to find a 4-cup teapot online, that is the right shape for this tea mitten. Now, I have very warm & cozy tea!
